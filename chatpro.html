Chat Rules and Regulations

  1. Only DIU students with valid DIU email addresses are allowed to participate.
  2. Be respectful to all participants - no harassment, bullying, or hate speech.
  3. No spamming or flooding the chat with repeated messages.
  4. Do not share personal information of yourself or others.
  5. No NSFW (Not Safe For Work) content of any kind.
  6. Admins have the right to remove messages and ban users who violate rules.
  7. Chat reactions (likes, etc.) are only available to approved users.
  8. Violations may result in temporary or permanent bans.
  9. Use the chat for its intended purpose - academic and social discussions.
  10. Have fun and keep the conversation positive!
